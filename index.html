About

At JSL Consulting, we believe structural engineering should be straightforward, not overcomplicated. Since 2009, we've been a trusted name in structural engineering across Nottingham and the Midlands, delivering clear, sensible solutions with a personal touch.

We are a responsive, client-focused team that delivers practical results without unnecessary complexity. Clients value our strong communication, commitment to deadlines, and hands-on service. From initial concept to final delivery, we're on hand when you need us, ready to listen, respond, and engineer with purpose.

Our dedicated team brings decades of experience, including work on globally recognised projects such as the British Library and Hong Kong Airport. We bring world-class expertise to every project, big or small. Today, we support residential, commercial, and public-sector clients across Nottingham and the Midlands with solutions tailored to the needs of each site, each build, and each budget.

We continue to push boundaries by combining engineering tradition with future technology and sustainable practices, ensuring future-ready results that stand the test of time. Straightforward structural engineering, practical solutions, and a team that's always on hand when you need us.
